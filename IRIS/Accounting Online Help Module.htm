Login to UP IRIS

1.       Go to the Admin button to login.

2.       Enter the required fields, Username and Password.

 

 

3.       Click the Admin login button and proceed.

 

 

4.       You have successfully logged in if you can see your modules or functions.

 

Editing Login Details

1.       Click on the option “Edit Login Details” to go to the page where you can update your login data.

2.       Update your login data by editing the fields shown on the page.

3.       When you are done changing your details, click the Save button to save.

4.       To cancel updating, click the Cancel button.

 

Editing User Profile

STUDENTS ACCOUNTABILITY MODULE

                The student’s accountability module for the Accounting Office is concerned with accountabilities that are related to student loans, scholarships, underassessment, and unpaid laboratory fees. This module allows the user to manage the accountabilities of students that are of the types stated earlier, functions include add, edit, clear, and view.

Add Accountability

1.       Click the Students Accountability Module, then click on Add Accountability.

2.       Enter the student number or last name of student on the input box then click the Submit button. 

 

3.       If student number is entered, fill up the fields, which ask for the details of the accountability you are adding then press the Save button.

 

 

4.       If last name is entered, possible entries of students with the last name entered will appear. Choose the correct student and press Use.

5.       Refer to no. 3: Fill up the fields, which ask for the details of the accountability you are adding then click the Save button.

 

Edit Accountability

1.       Click the Students Accountability Module.

2.       Enter the last name of the student on the search bar and click the Search button.

 

3.       Choose the correct student then click Edit.

 

 

4.       Update the details  you need to then click the Save button.

 

 

Clear Accountability

1.       Click the Students Accountability Module.

2.       Enter the last name of the student on the search bar and click the Search button.

 

3.       Choose the correct student then click Clear.

 

 

4.       Input the details on the fields required then click the Clear button.

 

 

 

ENROLLMENT MODULE

                The enrolment module for the Accounting Office primarily deals with the input of the student’s loanable amount into the system, assuming the Student Loan Board have approved the student’s application for student loan. Upon input, the total enrolment amount to be paid by the student (which is reflected on the student’s assessment of fees) will be subtracted by the loan therefore displaying the new total amount to be paid by the student.

 

Add Student Loan

1.       Click the Enrolment Module then choose Enroll Add SLB.

2.       Enter the student number of the student applying for the loan and click the Submit button.

 

 

3.       The student’s assessment page should contain the breakdown of fees. Enter the student loan amount then click on the Save button.

 

View Form 5

1.       Click the Enrolment Module then choose Enroll View Form 5.

2.       Enter the student number of the student applying for the loan and click the Submit button.

 

3.       This displays the Form 5 details of the student.

 

CASH REPORT Module

                The cash report module makes reporting all of the enrolment transactions more efficient. With just a few clicks, the user can instantly view a complete and printable report of all the transactions of the cash office pertaining to enrolment.

 

Generate Cash Report

1.       Click Cash Report Module then choose how you want to view the report.

2.       Click the Generate button.
