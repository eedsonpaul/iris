Online Help

(Student)

Editing Login account

On the left side of the student homepage, select edit login account from a list of functions.

After that, input the necessary information needed to complete the process. Then click update

 

Adding/Editing Personal Data

1.       When adding or editing Personal Data, select edit personal data from a list of functions on the left side of the student home page.

2.       Choose the correct semester and academic year.

3.       After being redirected to the next page, enter the correct data on the corresponding fields.  When you are done changing the details, click continue to proceed to the next page.

 

4.       Fill up the fields with the correct data and click on save to database to update your enrollment data.

Viewing Accountabilities

1.       On the left side of the student homepage, select view accountabilities from a list of functions.

2.       You will be redirected to the next page containing the details to your accountability.

Viewing grades per semester

1.       On the left side of the student homepage, select view grades per semester from a list of functions.

2.       Your grades will appear and you have the option to view the breakdown of your grades by clicking the view button under Action.

Adding a Subject

1.       On the left side of the student homepage, select register subject from a list of functions.

2.       A list of actions will appear and click on the add subject action.

3.       After being redirected, type the name of the subject you want to add and a list of schedules for the typed subject will appear.

4.        From there, select a suitable schedule and click Add

Removing a Subject

1.       When you have just finished adding a subject, the added subject will be shown and there will be two buttons accompanying it - confirm and remove. Click on the remove button to remove the subject from your schedule. If you have already confirmed the subject, the button unconfirm will appear beside it. Click on the unconfirm button, then click remove.
