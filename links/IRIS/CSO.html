Student Record Management

  1. Click on the ‘student record management’ option.
  2. To search for the student, enter in the text field either the student number, or the last name or the first name of the student. Then hit ‘GO’ or press Enter.
  3. The search results will appear in a new page.

 

  1. To add accountability
    1. Click on the ‘Accountability’ link (a). You’ll be redirected to a new page.
    2. Fill up the fields with the appropriate information then click the ‘ADD’ button.
  2. To enroll the student
    1. Click on the ‘Enroll’ link (b). You’ll be redirected to a page.

    1. Click the ‘ADD SUBJECT’ button to add some subjects for the student.
    2. Click ‘CHECK CONFLICT’ to view the student’s schedule, where any conflict is emphasized.
    3. Click ‘CHECK ACCOUNTABILITY’ to view the accountability/ies of the student.
    4. Click ‘EDIT ENROLLMENT DATA’ to view/edit/update the enrollment data of the student.
  1. To view/edit/update course
    1. Click on the ‘Course’ link (c). You’ll be redirected to a page.
    2. Fill up the fields with the appropriate data, then click ‘SAVE Student’s Course’ button.
    3. A message box will appear confirming success in editing/updating the course of the student. Click ‘OK’.
  2. To add/update graduation data
    1. Click on the ‘Graduation Data’ link (d). You’ll be redirected to a page.
    2. Fill up the fields with the appropriate data, then click ‘SAVE Student’s Graduation Data’.
    3. A message box will pop up confirming the addition or update of the graduation data of the student.
  3. To delete/remove account
    1. Click on the ‘Delete/Remove Account’ link (e).
    2. A message box will pop up affirming the success in removing the record of the student.
