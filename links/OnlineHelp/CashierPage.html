Login to UP IRIS

  1. Go to the Admin button to login.
  2. Enter the required fields, Username and Password.

  1. Click the Admin login button and proceed.

  1. You have successfully logged in if you can see your modules or functions.

Editing Login Details

  1. Click on the option “Edit Login Details” to go to the page where you can update your login data.
  2. Update your login data by editing the fields shown on the page.
  3. When you are done changing your details, click the Save button to save.
  4. To cancel updating, click the Cancel button.

Editing User Profile

  1. Click on the option Edit User Profile to go to the page where you can update your login data.

  1. Update your user profile data by editing the fields shown on the page.
  2. When you are done changing your details, click the Save button to save.

  1. To cancel updating, click the Cancel button.

 

 

 

 

 ENROLMENT MODULE
                This module of the cashier serves a critical role in the enrolment process. The cashier saves the amount paid by the student to the system.

Enroll Student

  1. Click on Enrolment Module.
  2. Then, click on Enroll Student.

  1. Enter the student number on the input field then press the Submit button.

  

  1. After step 3, the student’s assessment page should appear containing the breakdown of fees and the total amount to pay. Enter on the input fields the OR Number and Amount Paid then click the Save button.

STUDENTS ACCOUNTABILITY MODULE
                The students accountability module for the Cash office can only search and input payments for the student’s accountabilities and automatically clears them.

Input SLB Payment

  1. Click on Input SLB Payment.

 

 

  1. Enter the student number then click the Submit button.

  1. Click on Input Payment.
  2. Fill up the required fields OR number and Amount Paid then press the Clear button.
