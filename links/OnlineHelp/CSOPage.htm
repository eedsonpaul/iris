STUDENT’S CONCERN

 

Add Student Record

1.       From the navigation bars, click on the student’s concern.

2.       Choose the Add Student Record link.

3.       Enter the student number that you wish to add in the text field, then press ‘ADD’ button or press Enter.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 


4.       Supply information on fields with *. That means that the field is mandatory.

5.       After filling up, click the ‘ADD’ button at the bottom.

6.       A message box will appear confirming the status of the transaction. Click ‘OK’.

 

 

Student Record Management

 

1.       Still from the student’s concern navigation, select Student Record Management.

 

2.       To search for the student, enter in the text field either the student number or the last name of the student. Then hit ‘GO’ or press Enter.

 

3.       The search result will display all the students that passed the criteria inputted in the text field.

4.       Click on the name or the student number of the desired student record to view.

5.       A new page will appear where all the functions concerning the student are displayed.

a.       Edit student login account

1.  Click on the Login Account link (a). A new page will be opened.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

1.    Supply all the mandatory fields and click on the Click Here to Generate New Password link to generate a new password for the student.

2.    Press the UPDATE button after filling up.

b.      Edit student personal data

1.       Click on the Personal Data link. You will be redirected to a new page as shown below.

2.        Fill all the mandatory fields and then click UPDATE.

 

 

 

c.       Enroll student

1.  Click Enroll.

For further details on enrolling a student, refer to Enroll Student of General Registration Module.

d.      Edit maximum units

a.       Click Edit Max Units

b.      Input New Maximum Units

c.       Click Update button.

 

 

 

 

e.      Add accountability

1.       Click Accountability

2.       Input Accountability details

3.       Click Add Button to submit form.

f.        Edit Student Course

1.       Click Course

2.       Click Click Here To Change

 

3.       Choose Course and enter Academic Year and Semester and click Update Student’s Course.

g.       Edit student number

1.       Click Edit Student Number.

2.       Input new student number.

3.       Click Update.

 

 

 

h.      Add Graduation Data

1.       Click Graduation Data.

2.       Fill up form.

3.       Click SAVE Student’s Graduation Data.

GENERAL REGISTRATION MODULE

Enroll Student

1.       Enter a student number and click Go or just hit Enter key.

 

2.       A page same as the enroll link from the STUDENT’S CONCERN navigation is displayed.

a.       Add Subject

1.       Click on Add Subject.

2.       Refer to Add Subject of STUDENTS for further details on adding a subject.

b.      Edit Enrollment Data

1.    Click Edit Enrollment Data.

 

2.    Refer to Edit Enrollment Data of STUDENTS for further details on editing enrollment data.

c.       Print Form 5

1.    Click Print Form 5 or Print Paid Form 5.

2.    A printable version of the form 5 is displayed.

3.    Click Print Form 5.
