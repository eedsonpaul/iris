About UP IRIS

UP IRIS is an integrated online registration system. This is exclusive for the use of UP Cebu College students, faculty, and staff. 
The UP IRIS allows students to register for enrollment through online access.
Official updates released by the university can be viewed through the system.It also contains profiles and records of the people.

Borders of UP IRIS

The current status of UP IRIS is described as follows:

·         The system allows login of different users, namely:

o    Administrator

o    Student

o    Faculty

·         The system can add/edit/remove personal and login information for all users.

·         The system can register a student, which includes:

o    Add/Edit/Remove/Confirm/Unconfirm Subjects

o    View Grades and Schedule         

·         The Faculty/Staffs can monitor the enrollment process through the ff:

o    Register a Student

o    Add/Edit/Remove Class Offerings

o    Add/Edit/Remove Degree Programs

·         The Administrator can control the overall processes involved in enrollment through the ff:

o    Creating new accounts for all users

o    Do back-up Processes

o    Grant access and permissions

o    Override accounts of users
